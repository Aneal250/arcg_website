About Our Church

We are a Christ-centered family dedicated to spiritual growth, genuine fellowship, and transforming our world through the power of the Gospel. Here, every person is valued, every gift matters, and every soul has a place to call home.

Assembly of Redeemed Church of God

Assembly of Redeemed Church of God was founded in 1985 by a small group of dedicated believers who shared a vision for a church that would be deeply rooted in God's word while actively serving the community.

What began as a small gathering in a living room has grown into a vibrant congregation of believers from all walks of life. Through the years, we have remained committed to our founding principles while adapting to meet the changing needs of our community.

Today, we continue to build on the foundation laid by our founders, expanding our ministries and outreach efforts while maintaining a warm, welcoming environment where everyone can experience God's love.

Our Statement of Faith

These are foundational beliefs upon which our church is built

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit, whose indwelling enables Christians to live a godly life. We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit with the evidence of speaking in tongues and the manifestation of spiritual gifts.

The Bible

We believe the Bible to be the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God. It is our ultimate source of truth for Christian beliefs and living.

Salvation

We believe that salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone. His death on the cross provides the sole basis for forgiveness of sins and salvation, which is accessible to all who believe in Him

The Church

We believe in the church as the body of Christ, a community of believers who are called to worship God, grow in faith, and share the gospel with the world. We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, equal in power, glory, and honor.
